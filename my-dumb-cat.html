My Dumb Cat

And ode to the fluffy loser I share a home with.

I have had my dumb cat Cleyre since May 2016. She is pretty stupid, and I will tell you all of the reasons why:

  • She doesn't bury her own poop (WTF)
  • She sleeps all the time
  • She is very fluffy
    • Her hair is everywhere
    • I can no longer wear any dark colored clothing
  • She cries if there is a hole in her food bowl

She isn't all bad though. Here are some of my favorite things about her, in order:

  1. She lays on my chest when I got to bed and purrs until I fall asleep
  2. When she lays on the couch in a ball, she looks like a fluffy donut
